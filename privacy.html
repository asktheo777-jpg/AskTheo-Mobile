Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AskTheo ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up via Google or Email, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: We store your chat history, bookmarked verses, and reading preferences to synchronize your experience across devices.
  • Authentication Data: We use Supabase and Google OAuth to securely handle your login credentials. We do not store your passwords directly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to:

  • Provide and personalize the Bible reading and AI chat experience.
  • Maintain your "Wisdom Seeds" (credits) balance and subscription status.
  • Improve our AI models and application performance.

Note on AI: Your chat queries are processed by Google's Gemini AI API to generate responses. We do not sell your personal chat data to third parties for advertising.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, secure database access) to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We connect with the following third-party services:

  • Google: For authentication and AI processing.
  • Supabase: For database and backend services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this within the app settings or by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at: asktheo777@gmail.com
